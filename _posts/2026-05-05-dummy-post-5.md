The landscape of remote work has stabilized, but the tools we use continue to evolve.

## High-Quality Audio is Mandatory

A good microphone and noise-cancellation software are no longer optional. They are the professional equivalent of dressing well for the office.

## Asynchronous Communication

Fewer meetings, more structured documentation. Mastering async communication is the highest leverage skill a remote worker can learn.
