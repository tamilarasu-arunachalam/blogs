React Hooks have completely transformed how we write components. In this post, we'll explore some advanced patterns for creating reusable, highly-performant custom hooks.

## Why Custom Hooks?

Custom hooks allow you to extract component logic into reusable functions. This not only cleans up your components but also makes your logic highly testable.

## The `useFetch` Pattern

One of the most common requirements is data fetching. By building a robust `useFetch` hook, you handle loading states, error boundaries, and caching in one place.
