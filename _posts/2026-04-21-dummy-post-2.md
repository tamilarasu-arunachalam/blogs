Artificial Intelligence is no longer just a buzzword; it is an active participant in the coding process. From auto-completing complex functions to suggesting architectural improvements, AI is fundamentally changing the landscape.

## AI as a Pair Programmer

Tools like Copilot and advanced language models act as an always-on pair programmer, reducing the time spent on boilerplate code.

![AI Code](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1620712943543-bcc4688e7485?auto=format&fit=crop&q=80&w=1200)

## The Evolution of Debugging

Debugging is becoming faster. AI can pinpoint logical errors and suggest fixes before the code even runs in a test environment.
