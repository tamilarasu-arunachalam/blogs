## The Core Philosophy: Modular & Minimalist {#philosophy}

When designing my personal office, I follow three rules:
1. **Everything has a place.** If a tool doesn't have a designated spot, it's clutter.
2. **Invest in quality over quantity.** One premium monitor is better than three budget ones.
3. **Ergonomics is non-negotiable.** Your future self will thank you for that high-end chair.

> "The secret to successful remote work isn't just about the tools you use, but how you design the environment around them."

### Conclusion
